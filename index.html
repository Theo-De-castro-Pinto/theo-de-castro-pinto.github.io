About me

I am currently a PhD student, but aim to defend during 2025. My thesis is a conjoint work of LaBRI, Université de Bordeaux and Serma Safety and Security . The subject of my thesis is " Detecting physical vulnerabilities through automated binary analysis " and is supervised by Sébastien Ordas , Antoine Rollet and Grégoire Sutre.
